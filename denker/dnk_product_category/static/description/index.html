Denker - Product Category on Reports

Grupo Denker, www.grupodenker.com

Módulo para agregar la familia, subfamilia y color a modelos y reportes.

Modifica el Nombre a mostrar, para que no muestre las categorías padre.

Además agrega los siguientes campos:

    Product Category:
  • [dnk_abbreviation] - Abbreviation
    Invoice Report:
  • [dnk_subfamily_id] - Subfamily
  • [dnk_family_id] - Family
  • [dnk_color_id]- Color
    Sale Report:
  • [dnk_subfamily_id] - Subfamily
  • [dnk_family_id] - Family
  • [dnk_color_id]- Color
    Stock Move:
  • [dnk_subfamily_id] - Subfamily
  • [dnk_family_id] - Family
  • [dnk_color_id]- Color
    Stock Move Line:
  • [dnk_subfamily_id] - Subfamily
  • [dnk_family_id] - Family
  • [dnk_color_id]- Color
